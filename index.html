Subheading

SnapLens is designed to enhance the creativity, spontaneity, and social aspects of photography and video through innovative AR, AI-driven features, and deep integration with Snap Inc.s broader ecosystem. It caters to both casual users and professional content creators, offering something for everyone—from fun, interactive filters to advanced tools for crafting high-quality media.

Section Title

Snap-Reels: A mode dedicated to creating short-form videos for social sharing. Users can create multi-clip videos, adding different AR elements to each scene. Built-in editing tools let users adjust speed, add music, cut clips, and layer effects seamlessly. Snap Effects Store: A marketplace for user-generated effects, where users can sell or share their own AR filters, backgrounds, or video templates. Top creators can be highlighted for their work, encouraging community engagement and creativity.

Section Title

Direct integration with Snapchat, allowing users to instantly share photos or videos captured with SnapLens to their Snapchat stories or send them to friends, using the app’s chat or group functionality.
